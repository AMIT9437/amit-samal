What is GST?

The Goods and Services Tax (GST) is India’s unified indirect tax system, simplifying business compliance by merging multiple taxes under one structure. It ensures transparency, efficiency, and nationwide consistency in taxation.

Key Benefits:

  • Simplified tax structure and compliance
  • Eliminates cascading tax effects
  • Promotes transparency and accountability
  • Improves interstate trade flow
  • Reduces tax evasion
