What is MCA Compliance?

The Ministry of Corporate Affairs (MCA) regulates corporate entities in India. MCA compliance ensures companies follow the Companies Act, 2013 and maintain proper corporate governance standards.

Importance of MCA Compliance:

  • Legal requirement for all companies
  • Avoids penalties and legal issues
  • Maintains corporate reputation
  • Ensures transparency and accountability
  • Facilitates business growth and credibility

Types of Companies We Handle

Private Limited Company

Most popular choice for small to medium businesses

  • Minimum 2 directors
  • Maximum 200 shareholders
  • Limited liability protection
  • Easy transfer of shares

Public Limited Company

Suitable for large businesses planning to raise public funds

  • Minimum 3 directors
  • Unlimited shareholders
  • Can list on stock exchange
  • Higher compliance requirements

One Person Company (OPC)

Perfect for single entrepreneurs

  • Single shareholder
  • Single director
  • Limited liability
  • Simplified compliance

Limited Liability Partnership (LLP)

Hybrid structure combining partnership and company benefits

  • Limited liability for partners
  • Flexible management structure
  • Lower compliance burden
  • Tax efficiency
