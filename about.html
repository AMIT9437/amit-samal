Our Story

Freelancer Amit started as a small initiative to support businesses with their GST, ITR, and accounting needs — but we soon expanded our services to website development, creative posters, and event designs. Today, we proudly combine business solutions with digital innovation.

Our Vision

To become a one-stop destination for both business and web services — from filing your returns to building your online identity — we aim to empower freelancers, startups, and companies with professional support and creative designs.
